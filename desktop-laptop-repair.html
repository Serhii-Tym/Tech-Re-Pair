Common PC Issues FAQ

Q: What is the "Blue Screen of Death" (BSOD)? +
A: The BSOD usually indicates a critical system crash due to faulty drivers, hardware failure (RAM, disk), or an issue with the Windows operating system itself.
Q: How do I check my laptop's fan health? +
A: If the fan sounds louder than usual, or the laptop feels hot, use system monitoring software (like HWMonitor) to check your CPU/GPU temperatures. Dusting is often the solution.
Q: My PC turns on but the screen is black. +
A: This is a "No POST" issue. Check your monitor cable, ensure your graphics card is seated correctly, and try removing one stick of RAM at a time.
