Common Tablet Issues FAQ

Q: What causes screen discolouration? +
A: Discoloration can be caused by physical damage (pressure), liquid damage, or a failing display panel (LCD/OLED) itself.
Q: My touch screen is unresponsive. +
A: Try a hard reboot (check your model's specific button sequence). If it persists after a reset, the digitizer layer of the screen is likely damaged.
Q: Why is my tablet so slow? +
A: Over time, too many apps and a lack of storage can slow tablets down. Try deleting unused apps and clearing the app cache.
