Common Phone Issues FAQ

Q: My phone is stuck in a boot loop. +
A: This is usually a software issue. Try booting into recovery mode and clearing the cache or performing a factory reset.
Q: How much does a battery replacement cost? +
A: Battery replacement costs vary greatly by model and service center. Always check your manufacturer's certified repair partners first.
Q: Why is my camera blurry? +
A: Check if the lens is dirty or scratched. If not, the camera module may be physically damaged and require replacement.
